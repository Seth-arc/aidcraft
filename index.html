Hidden Debt Challenge

The coastal nation of Azuria has secured a commercial loan from China to build a major port facility, promising increased trade and economic growth. As repayment deadlines approach, the port is operating at only 40% capacity, falling far short of revenue projections. The Prime Minister has tasked you with finding a solution that preserves Azuria's fiscal stability without compromising diplomatic relationships.

Your initial analysis suggests the loan agreement contains concerning collateral clauses that could transfer control of this strategic asset to the lender in case of default. Meanwhile, local fishing communities report significant environmental damage from port dredging operations, adding political pressure.

As Finance Ministry Official, you must navigate complex stakeholder dynamics, evaluate refinancing options from both Chinese and Western institutions, and find a solution that protects your nation's financial stability, strategic assets, and political standing.
