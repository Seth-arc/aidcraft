About Debt & Diplomacy

Educational Purpose

Debt & Diplomacy is an educational simulation designed as a learning experience to help users understand the complex dynamics of development finance in emerging economies. The scenarios presented are based on real-world situations faced by many nations.

Key Learning Objectives

  • Understand debt sustainability challenges in infrastructure financing
  • Navigate complex stakeholder relationships including domestic and international actors
  • Balance economic development with environmental and social considerations
  • Experience the trade-offs involved in fiscal policy decision-making

Simulation Context

The fictional nation of Azuria represents a composite of real experiences from various countries that have engaged in large infrastructure projects with multiple financing sources. The challenges presented—from revenue shortfalls to environmental impacts—reflect documented case studies in development economics.

This simulation is designed for educational purposes and simplifies complex economic and political realities. It should be viewed as an introduction to these concepts rather than a comprehensive representation.
