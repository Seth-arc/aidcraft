The country is a lower-middle-income nation with significant natural resources but persistent development challenges. Recent political reforms have opened opportunities for international investment, but governance and transparency issues remain.

Development priorities include infrastructure modernization, particularly in transportation and energy sectors, to support economic growth and improved public services.
